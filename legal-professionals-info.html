  • Reasonable Calculation: When a defendant's only known identifier is their wallet address (common in crypto crimes), service to that wallet is the most reasonably calculated method to provide notice.
  • Actual Notice: Defendants actively monitor their crypto walletsâ€”they receive instant notifications of any transactions, ensuring actual notice.

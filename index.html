Step 1: Get a TronLink Wallet

What is TronLink?
TronLink is a free browser extension that acts as your secure digital wallet. It's like a digital ID that lets you sign and send legal documents on the blockchain.
For Desktop (Recommended)

Install the TronLink browser extension:

Chrome Extension TronLink Website
For Mobile

Download the TronLink app:

App Store Google Play
Security Tip: When you create your wallet, you'll get a "seed phrase" (12-24 words). Write this down and keep it safe - it's the only way to recover your wallet if you lose access.

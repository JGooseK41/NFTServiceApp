Common Questions

Why did I receive this?

Your wallet address was involved in a transaction or holds assets that are subject to legal proceedings. This could be related to asset recovery, civil litigation, regulatory compliance, or law enforcement action.

What happens if I ignore it?

Ignoring legal notices can have severe consequences including: default judgment against you, asset freezing or seizure, contempt of court charges, loss of your right to defend yourself, and additional fines and penalties.

Is this a scam?

This system is used by licensed process servers and law enforcement. You can verify authenticity by checking the sender's credentials, contacting the court directly, or consulting with a lawyer. The blockchain provides permanent proof of service.

Will signing cost me money?

No. Accepting the notice is free. The sender has already paid all fees. You're only acknowledging receipt, not agreeing to any terms or making any payment.

What should I do next?

1) Accept the notice to start the clock on your response time. 2) Read the document carefully. 3) Contact a lawyer immediately if criminal charges are involved. 4) Respond within the deadline specified. 5) Keep all documentation for your records.
